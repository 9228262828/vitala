Privacy Policy

Your Privacy Matters

Last Updated: July 22, 2026

Introduction

Vitala respects your privacy. This application is designed to work completely offline and does not require an account or internet connection to use its core features.


Information Stored

The application may store health records, medication lists, notes, symptoms, mood logs and personal settings only on your own device.

No information is transmitted to our servers.


No Data Collection

We do not collect personal information.

We do not create user accounts.

We do not upload your health records.

We do not sell or share any personal data.


No Advertising

Vitala contains no advertisements and does not use advertising identifiers or tracking technologies.


Permissions

The application only requests permissions when necessary for optional features available on your device.


Security

Because your information remains on your device, protecting your device with a screen lock, fingerprint or password is recommended.


Medical Disclaimer

Vitala is intended for personal health tracking only.

It is not a medical device and does not replace professional medical advice, diagnosis or treatment.


Children's Privacy

The application does not knowingly collect information from children.
